Training & consulting

EE source code is open, but it’s neither Open Source nor free to use. You can use it by purchasing a subscription plan of your choice. If your subscription finishes, you can still use and modify the source code of the last purchased EE version. However, in this case, you will stop getting further updates, can’t create new clusters, and are not allowed to provide Kubernetes-as-a-Service to third parties.
