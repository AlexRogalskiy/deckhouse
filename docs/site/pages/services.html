Managed Deckhouse

Built on top of Deckhouse Platform EE, Managed Deckhouse is a perfect choice if you:

  • want to get Kubernetes with zero effort;
  • have no plans for dedicated Kubernetes engineers and building own K8s expertise;
  • don’t consider managing Kubernetes platform the core competence of your business.

How does it work?

  • You provide us access to your infrastructure. It can be everything* on-premises or in the clouds.
    • * Supported options include AWS, GCP, Azure, OVH Cloud, OpenStack, VMware vSphere, bare-metal servers.
  • We design cluster’s architecture according to your needs, deploy and configure Deckhouse in your infrastructure.
  • We make upgrades for your clusters, monitor them, proactively solve any issues, and consult you. The maintenance of Deckhouse Platform is our responsibility.

What do I get?

  • Kubernetes clusters based on Deckhouse Platform EE (with all of its features) and fully managed by our team.
  • The best possible SLA. Flant’s engineers are responsible for all operations minimizing the probability of faults during maintenance.
  • Monitoring and critical incidents solving.
  • Consulting and support for your Kubernetes users.

Training & Consulting

Deckhouse training sessions

The sessions are designed to train your engineers in two directions:

  • How to fit Deckhouse into your architecture?
  • How to get the full power of Deckhouse?

Our approach implies an intense start (lots of information provided at the beginning of the training) and gradual application of new knowledge under the following supervision of our tutors.

Here are success packages for Deckhouse we provide:

Basic

2H×5
10-hours overview of Deckhouse platform: main features, installation, cluster deployment, update of the cluster and its components, set up monitoring, logging, and alerting.

Advanced

4H×5 + 2H×10
20-hours deep dive into Deckhouse platform to cover all the functionality of Deckhouse for growing teams. Tutoring sessions are followed by ten 2-hours sessions of Q&A, supervision, and guidance from the Flant team.

Premium

4H×10 + 2H×20
40 hours of sessions with customized agenda tied to your projects. Coverage of API, Commander, service mesh, and authentication modules along with the specific requirements from your side. Twenty 2-hours sessions to track your progress and adviseon your Kubernetes journey including performance issues.

Premium On-Site

4H×5 + 2H×10 + 3 days on-site
Premium package with additional 3 days of our DevOps engineer in an old-school classroom format of training. 40 hours of sessions followed by supervised hands-on experience on your premises with immediate feedback and guidance from Flant experts.
